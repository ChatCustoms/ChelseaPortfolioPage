About Me

Get to know my background, passion, and what drives me

Hello! I'm Chelsea Dawn Chatham

Detail-oriented Human Resources and Administrative professional with over 4+ years of experience supporting employee onboarding, personnel records, compliance, and customer service in fast-paced, highly regulated environments. In addition to her professional background, Chelsea is also a commissioned Florida Notary Public and Remote Online Notary (RON), offering reliable and convenient notarization services.

Chelsea brings demonstrated expertise in records management, data accuracy, policy adherence, and benefits coordination, with a strong commitment to confidentiality and ethical standards. Known for reliability, integrity, and the ability to manage sensitive information while delivering exceptional service to diverse clients and stakeholders.

Chelsea's proven leadership, problem-solving, and communication skills were developed through both military service and administrative roles. She brings the ability to prioritize tasks, adapt quickly, and perform effectively under pressure.

Building on this foundation of accuracy, confidentiality, and attention to detail, Chelsea now provides notary services for clients in Altamonte Springs, Orlando, and throughout Florida via Remote Online Notarization (RON). Her background in handling sensitive information and following strict procedures ensures every notarization is performed with professionalism and care.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration & Data Analysis at Liberty University, building on her Associate of Science in Business Administration from Colorado Technical University (GPA: 3.73).
