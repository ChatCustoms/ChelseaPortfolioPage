Frequently Asked Questions

What is Remote Online Notarization (RON)?

RON allows documents to be notarized online using a secure platform, so you do not need to meet in person if your document qualifies.

Do I need to be in Florida?

For in-person notarization, you must be local. For Remote Online Notarization, eligibility depends on the document and legal requirements.

What should I bring?

A valid government-issued ID and completed documents. Do not sign documents beforehand unless instructed.
